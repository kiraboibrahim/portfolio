PROJECTS

ANASHEED (Jan/2022 - Feb/2022)

Music is such a sensitive topic in the Islamic religion, but amidst all the mist around this topic, I decided to contribute to my religion through developing a service that allows sharing and streaming of anasheed music (special type of music). I divided the whole development into frontend and backend.

The frontend is hosted on gihub pages and the backend on pythonanywhere.com. As a source of data for the backend, I used scrapping to obtain information as I didn't find any available API to provide such kind of data.

I designed ANASHEED as a Progressive Web App (PWA) in order to allow a frictionless access to the entertainment.

KIRABOFLIX (March/2021 - May/2021)
